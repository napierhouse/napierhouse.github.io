Directions

Directions from South Coast M4

  1. Follow Durban City signs.
  2. Look for Moore Road (Che Guevera) Berea glide off to the left. You will come to a set of traffic lights 1st set.
  3. Continue straight from here and at the 6th set of traffic lights turn right into Cleaver Road.
  4. Follow straight on through a further 3 sets of traffic lights then at the 4th set glide off left into Sydenham Road (John Zikhali).
  5. Approximately 30m up Sydenham (John Zikhali) turn Right into Napier Road.
  6. The Bed & Breakfast is situated on the left hand side towards the middle of the block and is clearly marked.
  7. Please buzz the main house.

 

Directions from Johannesburg / Pietermaritzburg

  1. Proceed along the N3 towards Durban past the Pavilion Shopping complex. Look out for the Brickfield (Felix Dlamini) turn off.
  2. The next glide off onto Ridge Road (Peter Makoba) is the one to take. Follow straight on cross Essenwood Road (Stephen Dlamini) and then Musgrave Road.
  3. The following is Currie Road, turn left and proceed to the first stop sign, then turn right into Sydenham Road (John Zikhali).
  4. Continue down the hill and take the third street to the left which is Napier Road.
  5. The Bed & Breakfast is situated on the left hand side towards the middle of the block and is clearly marked.
  6. Please buzz the main house.

 

Directions from King Shaka International Airport / North Coast N2

  1. Proceed along the N2 South until the sign M19 Umgeni Road exit.
  2. Take this off ramp and proceed driving parallel to the N2 to the 2nd set of traffic lights.
  3. Turn left along Umgeni Road. Follow through several sets of Traffic lights till you reach Argyle Road (Sandile Thusi), large intersection.
  4. Turn right and follow up the hill keeping in the left hand lane till you see the sign Cowey Road (Problem Mkhize).
  5. Turn left at this set of lights and continue till you pass a garage and small shopping centre on the right, Cowey Centre.
  6. Take the next road to the right which is Lawrence then turn left into Napier Road.
  7. Napier House is situated on the right hand side clearly marked towards the middle of the block.
  8. Please buzz the main house.
