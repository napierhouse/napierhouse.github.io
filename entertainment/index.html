Entertainment

Durban is the Jewel in KwaZulu Natal’s Sunshine Coast. From our B&B there is much to do. We overlook the Greyville Racecourse, well known for the yearly Vodacom Durban July and other prestigious horse races. The Musgrave Shopping Mall situated just up the hill offers numerous shops, restaurants, a banking hall and cinema complex.

Durban’s Golden Mile, with the Sun Coast Casino, the new Ushaka Waterfront with aquarium are a mere 10 minutes drive away.

Enjoy a peaceful and quiet day at either Durban’s Botanical Garden or Mitchell Park which are both nearby. Mitchell Park is also home to a small zoo that makes a magic afternoon for adults and children alike.

For sporting occassions, we are within 4 kms of Durban’s ABSA Kings Park Stadium, home of the Natal Sharks and Kingsmead Cricket Stadium. Durban’s Country Club Golf Course and Beechwood Golf Course are in close proximity to the stadiums.

For that ideal meal, we are within 4 kilometers’ drive from over 50 of Durban’s best restaurants. From Greek to Chinese, Portuguese, Japanese, Indian, traditional African and many more to suit whatever your taste may be.
